The concept of universality of minimalism lies in the inclusion of various number of cases which are distinct characteristics
from the relational rules that are formed between the minimum parameters. In this way, the minimality of minimalism
includes the concept of universality and it’s the logic of ‘simplicity and diversity’ which means that when the parameter is
simpler, it creates more stories to tell. The minimalism does not always aims for simplifying or minimizing everything.
The minimalism’s tendency to simplify sometimes aims for various stories to tell.
